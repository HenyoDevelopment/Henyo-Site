About Henyo

Our organization formed in 2017 at the University of Maryland, College Park. Many people have the notion that in order to study computer science, you have to be smart in both technology and mathematics. Here at Henyo, we want to inspire those who have a passion to learn about the field and that you only need to be interested.

We aren't interested in what major you are currently, whether it is Computer Science, Information Science, or English. Our interest is those who wish to learn more about the field. Everyday we help our members develop the skills necessary to be able to develop projects they envision.
